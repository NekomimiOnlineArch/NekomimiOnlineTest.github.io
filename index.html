    • @item (id/name) - Spawns item id/name
    • @blevel (number) - Increases Base level by number. Can use negatives as well.
    • @jlevel (number) - Increases Job level by number. Can use negatives as well.
    • @spawn (id/name) - Spawns monster.
    • @job (name) - Changes to named job.
    • @zeny (amount) - Increases Zeny.
    • @killmonster - kills all monsters on field.
    • @go (number/map name) - Warp.
    • @warp (map name) - Warp to specific map.
    • @refine (item id) (refine value) - Auto refines.
    • @heal/@revive - self explanatory.
    • @homlevel (+/- amount) - Increase Homonculus level.
    • @homlevel (+/- amount) - Increase Homonculus level.
    • @homfriendly (0-1000) - Sets the intimacy level of your homunculus, with 1000 being "Loyal".
    • @petfriendly (0-1000) - Sets the intimacy level of your pet, with 1000 being "Loyal".
